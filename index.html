NER on Job Descriptions Using spaCy
spaCy NER

Welcome!!

This is a comprehensive tutorial on how to perform Named Entity Recognition (NER) on job descriptions using spaCy. This tutorial provides step-by-step instructions on how to install and use spaCy to identify and categorize named entities such as people, organizations, locations, and dates in a job description. The tutorial also includes examples and tips on customizing the NER model for your specific use case. By following this tutorial, you will be able to leverage the power of spaCy to extract valuable information from job descriptions and enhance your recruitment and hiring process.
